About Pauline

My name is Pauline Laurent, I’m 25 years old and currently working at Pascale Venot, a PR agency located in Champs Elysées, Paris. I have been working as an Influence Project Manager for over two years, during which I had the opportunity to collaborate with more than 40 different brands from various industries, including hospitality, restaurants, consumer brands, and decoration.

Through these experiences, I have developed strong project management skills, enabling me to effectively oversee and execute diverse and complex campaigns. These experiences allowed me to build and maintain strong relationships with clients and influencers, ensuring mutually beneficial partnerships. Besides that, I have gained expertise in strategic planning and execution, from creating strategies to developing detailed retroplanning.
