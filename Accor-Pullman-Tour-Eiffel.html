Accor Group: Pullman Tour Eiffel

Project Overview

Pullman Tour Eiffel, a luxury hotel in Paris, hosted an exclusive 14th of July party on its rooftop, offering unparalleled views of the Eiffel Tower. The event aimed to drive ticket sales through strategic influencer collaborations, leveraging the unique setting to create captivating content.

Main Tasks

  • Influencer Negotiation: Secured partnerships with influencers for non-paid collaborations, encouraging them to create content with sales links to drive ticket purchases.
  • Content Strategy: Developed a strategy for influencers to produce engaging content that highlighted the event’s unique location and experience.
  • Post-Campaign Reporting: Provided detailed analysis of the campaign's performance, focusing on sales metrics and influencer impact.

Results

Sold-Out Event: The event achieved full ticket sales despite having no budget for paid influencer promotions, demonstrating the effectiveness of the strategy.

Content Creation: Influencers generated a wealth of engaging content, significantly boosting the event's visibility and enhancing Pullman's brand prestige.

  • Influencers: 15
  • Content: 17 stories, 2 reels, 1 IG post
  • Estimated Impressions: 1.9M
  • Earned Media Value: 90K€
